About This Tool

This cash counter helps shopkeepers, cashiers, and businesses quickly tally their daily cash collections. Simply enter the count of each denomination and get the total instantly. Perfect for closing registers and cash reconciliation.

Did You Know? Indian Currency Facts

₹1 Note: It is the only currency note signed by the Finance Secretary of India, not the RBI Governor.
₹0 Note: An organization called '5th Pillar' created zero rupee notes to fight corruption and bribery!
Alien Notes? Notes issued before 2005 are being withdrawn. Check the year printed on the back!
Material: Indian notes are not paper! They are made of cotton and cotton rag.

Indian Currency Denominations

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) currently issues currency notes in denominations of ₹10, ₹20, ₹50, ₹100, ₹200, and ₹500. The ₹2000 note was discontinued in 2023 but remains legal tender.

How to Use This Cash Counter

  • Step 1: Count and separate your cash by denomination
  • Step 2: Enter the number of notes/coins for each denomination
  • Step 3: The total amount is calculated automatically
  • Step 4: Use Reset All to clear and start a new count

This tool is especially useful for retail businesses, small shops, and anyone handling cash transactions daily. It eliminates manual calculation errors and speeds up the cash tallying process.
