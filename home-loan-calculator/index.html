Understanding Home Loans in India

What is a Home Loan?

A home loan (also called housing loan or mortgage) is a secured loan provided by banks and financial institutions to help you purchase, construct, or renovate a residential property. The property itself serves as collateral, and you repay the loan through fixed monthly installments (EMIs) over 5-30 years.
