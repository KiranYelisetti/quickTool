Plan Your Child's Foreign Education

Planning for your child's foreign education requires considering two critical factors that most parents overlook: education cost inflation and currency depreciation. Our Study Abroad Calculator helps you understand the true future cost of foreign education.

Key Features

How to Use

  1. Select your preferred study destination
  2. Enter the current total cost for a 4-year degree (including living expenses)
  3. Set the number of years until your child begins studying
  4. Adjust inflation and depreciation rates based on your assumptions
  5. View the projected future cost and required monthly investment
