About QuickTools India

India's trusted platform for free, privacy-first financial calculators and everyday tools

Our Mission

QuickTools India was founded with a clear mission: to democratize financial planning for every Indian — whether you're a first-generation investor in a small town or a seasoned professional in a metro city. We believe that access to accurate, easy-to-use financial calculators should not require expensive software, sign-ups, or sharing your personal data.

Every tool we build runs 100% in your browser. Your numbers stay on your device. We don't ask for your phone number, email, or any personal information. We simply want to help Indians make better financial decisions — quickly and privately.

What Makes Us Different

Most online calculators give you a basic EMI result and stop there. We go deeper — because real financial decisions require real context:

Home Loan Calculator

Shows pre-payment impact (how ₹1 Lakh extra/year saves ₹15-20 Lakhs in interest), tax benefits under Section 24(b)/80C/80EEA, and bank rate comparisons — features that generic calculators skip entirely.

Car Loan Calculator

Goes beyond EMI to show complete on-road price including RTO tax, insurance, and accessories. Compares new vs used car economics so you don't end up "underwater" on your loan.

Investment Calculators

SIP, PPF, SSY calculators with inflation adjustment, step-up SIP projections, and year-wise growth tables — helping you visualize long-term wealth creation.

Tax & Salary Tools

Income Tax Calculator comparing Old vs New Regime, CTC-to-In-Hand Salary Calculator, and GST Calculator with CGST/SGST breakdown — all updated for FY 2025-26.

Our Methodology & Accuracy

We take accuracy seriously. Every calculator on QuickTools India is built following a rigorous process:

1

Research & Source Verification

All formulas are sourced from official RBI guidelines, SEBI regulations, Income Tax Act provisions, and government scheme documents. We cross-reference interest rates with official bank and post office publications.

2

Mathematical Validation

Every calculation is validated against known results from bank EMI calculators, EPFO projections, and manually computed spreadsheets. We test edge cases (zero values, maximum limits, boundary conditions).

3

Regular Updates

When government policies change (new tax slabs, revised PPF/SSY rates, updated GST rules), we update our calculators and educational content promptly to reflect the latest regulations.
