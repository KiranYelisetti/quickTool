About GST

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services in India. CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST) are applied for intra-state transactions, while IGST (Integrated GST) is for inter-state transactions.

GST Rate Slabs & Common Items 2025

Goods and Services Tax (GST) in India is categorized into 5 main slabs. Knowing the correct slab is crucial for accurate calculation.

Rate Category Common Items
0% (Nil) Essential Goods Fresh vegetables, milk, bread, salt, newspaper, education services
5% Household Essentials Sugar, tea, coffee, edible oil, spices, life-saving drugs, transport services (Rail/Air Economy)
12% Standard Goods Computers, processed food, mobiles (some), business class air travel
18% Standard Services & Goods Restaurants, IT services, hair oil, soap, toothpaste, capital goods, most services
28% Luxury & Sin Goods Automobiles, ACs, refrigerators, tobacco products, cement, aerated drinks

Special Rates

  • 0.25%: Rough diamonds and precious stones
  • 3%: Gold, silver, and jewelry
