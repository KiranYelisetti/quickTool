Understanding Indian Number System

The Indian numbering system uses unique terms like Lakh and Crore that differ from the International system. This converter helps you understand and convert between both systems effortlessly.

Common Conversions

Indian System:

  • Thousand = 1,000
  • Lakh = 1,00,000 (Hundred Thousand)
  • Crore = 1,00,00,000 (Ten Million)
  • Arab = 100 Crores (used informally)

International System:

  • Thousand = 1,000
  • Million = 1,000,000 (10 Lakhs)
  • Billion = 1,000,000,000 (100 Crores)
  • Trillion = 1,000 Billions
